heiwa - 平和 (へいわ) : a noun meaning 'peace' in Japanese.

located in the heart of shoreditch, we are an art gallery, shop & an institute of heiwa.
we have three beautiful exhibition spaces where we showcase local & international artists/photographers. our aim is to invoke a feeling of peace for all of our visitors, as we believe this to be of the utmost importance in contrast to the busy lives we lead.

for information regarding current and future exhibitions see here.

to take some of this peace home with you, head to our shop where you will find artwork & prints on sale from some our previous exhibition artists.

to keep up to date with our latest exhibitions information and product releases sign up to our newsletter!
